# About

Keyri provides passwordless multi-factor authentication and fraud protection workflows, seamlessly integrating into your apps for a frictionless digital customer experience.

Our tools enable user authentication and device fingerprinting for mobile and web applications. By incorporating our mobile SDKs and web packages, you can:

- Implement secure, phishing-resistant ["Sign in with QR" functionality](/authentication/qr-authentication) across multiple platforms
- [Authenticate users in your mobile app](/authentication/mobile-app-authentication) using biometrics, eliminating the need for passwords
- Cryptographically link users' phones to their accounts, enabling automatic background authentication
- [Fingerprint](/device-fingerprinting) users' [mobile devices](/device-fingerprinting/mobile-fingerprinting) and [web browsers](/device-fingerprinting/web-fingerprinting) to detect and block malicious activity, as well as enhance user experience and reduce your 2FA cost
